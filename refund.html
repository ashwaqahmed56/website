Refund Policy

Learning Leaders EdTech Academy

Please review our refund policy carefully before purchasing any course.

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

At Learning Leaders EdTech Academy, we strive to provide high-quality courses and a positive learning experience. However, we understand that sometimes refunds are necessary. This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be granted.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests will be considered under the following conditions:

  • The request is made within 7 calendar days of the original purchase date.
  • The course has not been completed or accessed beyond 20% of the total content.
  • There are valid reasons such as technical issues that prevent access to the course.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@example.com within the refund window.
  2. Provide your purchase details, including order number, course name, and reason for the refund request.
  3. Allow up to 7 business days for our team to review and respond to your request.

3. Refund Process

If your refund is approved:

  • The refund will be issued using the original payment method.
  • Refund processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

  • Requests made after the 7-day refund window.
  • Course access exceeds 20% of the content.
  • Change of mind or dissatisfaction with course content after accessing it.
  • Failure to comply with our Terms and Conditions.

5. Technical Issues

If you encounter technical problems accessing a course, please contact our support team immediately. We will make reasonable efforts to resolve the issue or provide alternative access before considering a refund.

6. Cancellation of Courses

In the rare event that we cancel a course, enrolled students will be offered a full refund or credit towards another course.

Note: Our refund policy is designed to be fair to all students and instructors. We encourage you to carefully consider your enrollment before making a purchase.
